# Amazon Connect Customer now supports scheduling tasks up to 90 days in advance

<p>Amazon Connect Customer now supports scheduling tasks up to 90 days in advance, helping organizations plan, route, and track long-running follow-up work. For example, an insurance team managing an auto repair claim can schedule future tasks for an adjuster visit, parts availability check, and repair completion follow-up, with each task routed to the right team at the right time with relevant claim context. You can schedule tasks using the StartTaskContact API, flows, or the agent workspace.</p> 
<p>This feature is available in all commercial and AWS GovCloud (US) regions where Amazon Connect Customer is offered. To learn more, see our <a href="https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/tasks.html">documentation</a>. To learn more about Connect Customer, visit the <a href="https://aws.amazon.com/products/connect/customer/">Amazon Connect Customer website</a>.&nbsp;</p>
